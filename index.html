Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our services

What are your shipping timelines?
Our shipping timelines vary based on the service: Express (1-2 days), Priority (2-3 days), and Standard (4-7 days). International shipments typically take 7-14 business days depending on the destination country and customs clearance.
How do I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment using the Tracking page on our website. Simply enter your AWB (Air Way Bill), Forwarding Reference Number, or Reference Number. You'll receive real-time updates on your shipment status. You can also enable SMS/Email notifications for tracking updates.
What items are restricted for shipping?
We cannot ship hazardous materials, explosives, flammable liquids, or prohibited items as per international regulations. For a detailed list of restricted items, please contact our support team or visit our policies section.
What is volumetric weight?
Volumetric weight is calculated based on the dimensions of your shipment. For surface shipping: (Length × Width × Height) ÷ 27,000 × 7 = Volumetric weight. You'll be charged the higher of volumetric or actual weight. Use our calculator to determine your billable weight.
Do you offer insurance for shipments?
Yes, we offer optional shipment insurance coverage. You can insure your package against loss or damage during transit. The insurance premium is calculated based on the declared value of your shipment. Contact our sales team for more details.
What are your payment options?
We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, NEFT, RTGS, and digital wallets. Corporate clients can opt for monthly invoicing and payment on account basis. Contact our billing team for more information.
What is your refund policy?
We offer full refunds if the shipment is cancelled before pickup. If cancelled after pickup, applicable charges will be deducted. For failed deliveries, refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. Please refer to our complete cancellation and refund policy for detailed terms.
Do you provide warehousing services?
Yes, we offer professional warehousing and storage solutions. Our facilities are climate-controlled, secure, and equipped with inventory management systems. We provide short-term and long-term storage options. Contact our team for rates and availability.

Our Journey

Founded in 2002, EPS Worldwide has grown from a regional courier service to a comprehensive logistics provider serving businesses across India and globally. Our success is built on a foundation of reliability, transparency, and customer-centric service.

We began with a simple mission: to provide dependable, cost-effective shipping solutions for businesses of all sizes. Over two decades, we've expanded our services to include international courier, cargo management, warehousing, and specialized logistics solutions.

Today, EPS Worldwide partners with leading carriers including BlueDart, GATI, and DTDC, leveraging their networks while maintaining our own quality standards. Our experienced team of logistics professionals brings deep industry knowledge and a commitment to excellence to every shipment.

What sets us apart is our attention to detail, personalized service approach, and willingness to customize solutions for each client's unique needs. Whether you're shipping a single parcel or managing bulk commercial consignments, we treat every shipment with equal care and professionalism.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation and refund procedures

1. Cancellation Before Pickup

Shipments can be cancelled free of charge before pickup from the sender's location. To cancel, contact our customer service team with your shipment tracking number.

2. Cancellation After Pickup

If a shipment is cancelled after pickup, applicable charges will be deducted from the refund amount. The deduction includes handling, fuel, and courier charges.

3. Refund for Failed Delivery

If a shipment cannot be delivered after 3 delivery attempts, the shipment will be returned to the sender. Refunds for failed deliveries are processed within 7-10 business days.

4. Lost or Damaged Shipments

If a shipment is lost or damaged in transit, compensation is limited to the declared value or Rs. 500, whichever is less, unless additional insurance was purchased. Claims must be filed within 15 days of the incident.

5. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. The refund will be credited to the original payment method used.

6. Non-Refundable Charges

Certain charges such as insurance premiums and special handling fees are non-refundable once the shipment has been picked up.

7. Special Requests

For refund requests not covered under this policy, please contact our customer service team for individual evaluation.

8. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at Info@epsworldwide.in with your shipment tracking number and reason for refund. Include any supporting documentation if applicable.

Shipping Policy

Learn about our shipping process and delivery timeframes

1. Shipping Methods

Express Service: 1-2 business days delivery (additional charges apply)
Priority Service: 2-3 business days delivery

We provide delivery to all major cities and towns across India. For remote areas, additional delivery time may be required. International delivery is available to 100+ countries.

3. Pickup Service

We offer free pickup for shipments above 5 kg. For lighter shipments, pickup can be arranged for a nominal charge. Pickup is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

4. Delivery Time Guarantee

While we strive to meet delivery timelines, unforeseen circumstances may cause delays. We are not liable for delays due to weather, natural disasters, or customs clearance.

5. Proof of Delivery

Our delivery executives obtain recipient signatures and provide photographic evidence of delivery. Delivery confirmation is sent via SMS and email.

6. Volumetric Weight Calculation

For express and surface shipments, volumetric weight is calculated as: (Length × Width × Height) ÷ 27,000. You pay for whichever is higher - volumetric weight or actual weight.

7. Restricted Items

The following items cannot be shipped: hazardous materials, explosives, flammable liquids, weapons, drugs, and items prohibited under law. Please refer to our prohibited items list for complete information.

8. Insurance Coverage

Basic liability coverage is included with all shipments (up to Rs. 500). Additional insurance can be purchased for high-value items at competitive rates.

9. Tracking Your Shipment

All shipments come with a unique tracking number. You can track your shipment in real-time using our website or mobile app. SMS updates are sent at each stage of delivery.
